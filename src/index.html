What is Dexilon

Decentralized Exchange to trade Perpetual Swaps

Dexilon is the first in kind DEX that is run on limit order book powered by the native blockchain

Governed by
Decentralized Organization

Democratically managed protocol by the community in a trustless and verifiable way.

Key Features

Decentralised Perpetual
Swap contracts

Market efficiency as funding rates combined with rebates will incentivise arbitrageurs.

Decentralised Limit
Order Book

Our decentralised limit order book is run on dedicated blockchain and able to perform hundred thousand of TPS without risk of congestion.

Self-custody and
Permissionless

Customers connect crypto-wallet and lock their funds on the smart contract just like typical DEX.

Transparent and Auditable
Risk Management

Aspects like liquidation and min margin are open source and verifiable.

Attractive Fees

Rebate for makers will result in liquidity blow up and deep order book. Taker fees will be transparently distributed towards makers and insurance fund.

Decentralized Data Oracles

Price oracles will supply real-time prices of public stock to form the index price of the underlying stock.

What is Dexilon
0
1

Decentralised Derivatives Exchange

Dexilon aims to provide cheapest and most scalable decentralised trading experience, by combining the most efficient and cheap blockchains as a settlement and order matching infrastructure

Governed by Decentralized Organization

Democratically managed protocol by the community in a trustless and verifiable way.

Key Features
0
2

Perpetual Swap Contracts on Stocks and Crypto

Market efficiency as funding rates combined with rebates will incentivise arbitrageurs.

Self-custody and Permissionless

Users connect crypto-wallet and lock their funds on the smart contract just like typical DEX.

On-chain settlement; Multi-chain order matching engines

Trade settlement will run on Polygon blockchain, while matching engines will be distributed across multiple blockchains, ensuring cheapest order creation and cancel.

Fees redistribution between market participants

Taker fees will be transparently distributed towards rebate for Maker orders and Insurance Fund.

Decentralized Data Oracles

Price oracles will supply real-time data to form the index price of the underlying asset.

DAO and DXLN Token

DXLN token will allow holders to participate in the governance of the protocol, vote and propose changes.
